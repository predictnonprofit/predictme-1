Do I have to have a Predict Me Profile?
Yes. Every user must have a profile on Predict Me. You can edit and change your user profile as you deem appropriate.
How do I sign up for Predict Me?
It’s simple. Visit www.PredictMe.com and create an account. We will send you an email requiring you to verify your account.
Logging in vs. signing up: what’s the difference?
If you don’t have an account on Predict Me, we will require you to register/create an account. (Visit www.PredictMe.com to create an account) If you have an existing account on Predict Me, you only need to login in to access your account (To log in, enter your email address and password).
How do I update my user profile?
To update information in your user profile, please visit: www.Predict Me.com
Enter your email address and password to sign in. You can update/edit:
  • Profile Information
  • Subscription Information
  • Billing Information
Don’t have a Predict Me profile? Register now by going to www.Predict Me.com
Can I save results and reports pages in CSV and PDF?
Yes. After the model is run, you can download your predicted data into the CSV file, download the model summary in pdf. There’s also a function for PDF creation to create PDF files of your reports/dashboard.
How do I change my password?
To change password:
  • Sign in
  • Go to your profile section
  • Select Change Password and update your password
I forgot/never received my username and/password. How do I retrieve it?
To get your username or password:
  • Go to http://www.PredictMe.com.
  • Go to the login/sign up page
  • Click forgot password
  • Enter the email address associated with your Predict Me account and click send.
We will send you an email with instructions to reset your password. For security reasons, the link will expire in 2 hours. If you do not receive the email from Predict Me within 2 hours, please check your spam and junk folders.
I can’t remember my username. What should I do?
Please go to www.PredictMe.com to recover your username by entering your email address. If you can't remember your email address, contact us by filling out the Contact Us form, and we will get back to you within 48 hours. If your organization provides your access to Predict Me, contact your account administrator or the person who opened the Predict Me account.
I submitted a request to reset my password, but I have received an email with the instruction? How do I go about it?
Check your junk and spam folders for a message from contact@PredictMe.com
What are the passwords requirements for Predict Me?
Predict Me works to protect your security and privacy with strong password requirements. Your Predict Me password must:
  • Contain at least six characters
  • Include at least one letter and one number
  • Not repeat the same character more than two times in a row
  • Not include white spaces
Why did I receive a confirmation email from Predict me?
When you sign up for a Predict Me account, we will send you a confirmation email. This way, we can make sure that you own the email address connected to your account. To confirm this, all you need to do is click on the link in the email.
I received an error message
We require that your browser accept cookies to log in. Please enable your browser’s cookies and then close and restart your browser to log in. Once you allow sites to accept cookies, close and restart your browser. If you still receive the error message, check the clock on your computer. Make sure the date and time are correct, and then log in again. This should resolve the problem.
I haven’t received my Predict Me sign up confirmation email. What should I do?
Your email server may have blocked the confirmation email. If you are in a corporate environment and can't check email in a spam folder, check with your administrator to see if the confirmation can be resent.
Do I need special equipment or software to create a Predict Me profile?
If you can view this page, you have all the necessary equipment and software to create your organizational profile.
How reliable are your servers?

My Predict Me membership is about to expire. What should I do?
When your Predict Me membership is about to expire, we'll send you a friendly email reminder. Predict Me memberships are set to renew automatically, so unless you cancelled your account, you won’t need to do anything.
How can I get a copy of my payment receipt?

What is your cancellation policy? Can I cancel anytime, or am I locked in for a certain amount of time?
Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time. You will have access to the membership until the end of your billing cycle, at which time your membership will not renew. When you are ready to reactivate your membership, just go to www.Predict Me.com, sign in, and it will prompt you to reactivate your membership.
How do I update my credit card and/or billing information?
To change/update your billing address or credit card information:

  • Enter your new information in the appropriate fields
  • Select “Save” to confirm changes

Is there a minimum number of records required to run donor predictions?
Yes. It all depends on your plan. Some plans have fewer or more records than others.
Do I know the number of records uploaded before running the model?
Yes. Our data handler counts the records before running the model. Once you upload your file, the application will show you the number of records in the file. You can make as many changes as you wish before running the model. This will also depend on your subscription plan.
What is the output after uploading my donor data file?
In addition to your data, you will receive:
  • Donor file (CSV) with prediction details. (the report will include model name and probability score for each record)
  • Model fit - summary report in PDF
  • Your dashboard will help explain your data, predictions, and other metrics
How does monthly subscription and data limit work?
Your monthly subscription cycle starts from the day you start your plan and ends after 30 days. It auto-renews every 31 days. During the 30 days, you can either use all your data limit on the day you sign up to run your predictive model or use the data in batches until you hit your data limit. For example, you start your subscription model on January 1st, and you select 200 data limits per month. Based on the data limit, you can run the entire data (total of 200) on the day you sign up or within 30 days, or you could run them in batches. For instance, you could run the first 50 data records on the 1st day and 100 data records on the 5th day and the remainder 50 data records on the 30th day. It depends on how you use it.
Will my unused data limit rollover to the next month?
No. Your data limit expires after 30 days. You must use/run all data limits within 30 days. If you continue with your subscription plan, your data will auto-reset. Any unused data will expire and will not be rolled over to the next month.
Do you refund unused data?
No. We don’t have a refund policy for unused data. We advise that you use/run all data limits within 30. For more information, refer to our Refund Policy and Payment terms.
What if I used up all my data before the 30 days? I still need to run a predicting model on a new donor file. How do I go about it?
You have two options.
Option 1:
Use the custom cost option to run a new donor file. For example, if you used all your 200 data limits and you want to run the new file, upload the file, choose the one-time option, and run the model. You will be charged per record on the new file.
Option 2:
Wait for the next monthly cycle.
Do I have access to results and profiles after my subscription has expired?
No. Donor profiles and results are only active during an active subscription. If you cancel or your subscription expires, you will have to renew your subscription to gain access to the profile and results.
How safe is my donor data?
At Predict Me, we take privacy and security very seriously. Our online platform uses AWS and undergoes regular internal and external security testing to ensure that all donor data is secure.
