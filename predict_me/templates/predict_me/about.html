About Us

Who is Predict ME

What is Predict Me?

Predict Me is a web-based application that focuses on providing donation likelihood prediction based on a donor’s historical data. Our intelligence solution uses data science analytics and algorithms to provide an optimized probability of a donor to donate.

Don’t work for your donor data; let data work for you!

We will analyze your demographic patterns, educational levels, donor giving trends, and other important variables to predict future donations.

Our main goal is to help all our clients make substantial, distinctive, and lasting improvements in their performance to fuel their success story. We aim to connect nonprofits, charities and NGOs with the right people that care about their cause.

Uncover the trends and patterns hiding within your donor datasets – identify who is willing to donate.

Revolutionize your fundraising through data science and analytics!

Our Ideal Clients

So, who is Predict Me for? If you are in the fundraising industry, then this tool is for you! Any entity that accepts individual donations and has an individual donor can use this application to predict the likelihood of donors who will donate in the future.

Some of our leading clients’ niches include:

  • Human and social services
  • Religion-related organizations
  • Arts and culture
  • Education-related entities
  • Health and hospitals
  • International organizations
  • Environmental and animals related organizations
