How Predict Me Works



Your organization is not any other typical nonprofit – we understand that your needs are unique! At Predict Me, we are proud to offer top technology for nonprofits and NGOs.

Our predictive modeling application uses 10 best statistical classification methods, including Random Forest Classifier and Logistic Regression. These methods were chosen based on the performance of their statistical tests and accuracies.

Our main goal at Predict Me is to create a solution where you can upload a dataset of donors and non-donors and receive your prediction in an instant! We are here to maximize your potential – we will help you strengthen your strategy, discover the best practices, implement solutions to enhance efficiency and engagement!

Once you upload your data, Predict Me will pre-process the dataset and train multiple different classification algorithms/models on your data. After the processing, you will be able to download the dataset alongside a report that can be used for your next fundraising campaign. The report includes the likelihood probabilities of donating.

Steps Involved in Our Predictive Modeling

A. What You Need to Do

Upload your donor file, and then relax…we will do everything else for you!

1. Data cleansing and validation of the input data file
2. Identification of textual data and categorical columns
3. Feature importance calculation
4. Testing and training input data for statistical classification methods
5. Deploying predictive results and executing insights
